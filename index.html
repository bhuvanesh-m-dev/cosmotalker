🌱 CosmoTalker Uses Ecosia Search

CosmoTalker has a built-in search feature that can use Ecosia — a special search engine that plants trees every time someone searches the web.

What is Ecosia?

Ecosia is a search engine just like Google or Bing, but with a beautiful twist: it uses most of its profits to plant trees and fight climate change. When people search the internet using Ecosia, the money made from ads goes into real tree-planting projects around the world. 🌳🌍

As of now, Ecosia has helped fund the planting of hundreds of millions of trees in places that need forests and green life the most — helping restore ecosystems, absorb carbon dioxide, and support people and wildlife.

Why this matters:

  • 🌿 Every time someone uses the CosmoTalker search feature with Ecosia, they are contributing to tree planting and a greener planet.
  • 🌎 This means CosmoTalker is not only about learning space — it also supports positive environmental action here on Earth.
"CosmoTalker helps you learn and explore and helps plant trees when you search — because its search uses Ecosia, the search engine that gives back to the planet."
